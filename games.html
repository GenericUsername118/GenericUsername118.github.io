My Favorite Games

Team Fortress 2 is a team-based first person shooter game, released in October of 2007. In the game, the player has nine different "classes" to choose from, which are characters which have widely different playstyles from one another. The game features multiple different gamemodes, including Capture The Flag (CTF), King of The Hill (KOTH), and Capture Points (CP).

Risk of Rain 2 is a third-person "rougue-like" game developed by Hopoo Games. As a rogue-like game, it has a relatively simple game loop which conssits of short, randomized runs (usually between half an hour to an hour). In the game, the goal is to collect items throughout the run to make your character stronger, while eventually escaping the planet that you're on.

Enter The Gungeon is another rogue-like game, developed by Dodge Roll and published by Devolver Digital. In the game, you have venture through the "Gungeon", a gun-themed dungeon. During this, you collect guns & gun-themed items in chests, fight gun-themed enemies, and try to make it to the (gun-themed) final boss.

Rainbow Six Siege is a team-based shooter game developed by Ubisoft. In it, a team of five works together to fortify and protect an objective within a building, while another team of five attempts to breach their defenses and reach the objective.
